How to make creamy tomato pasta

Ingredients

  • Some pasta
  • Some cream
  • Some Tomato paste
  • An onion
  • Chicken pieces

Cooking

  1. Cook the onions and chicken
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Add cream and tomato paste to the onions and chicken
  4. Season until taste yummy
  5. Combine the cooked pasta and sauce with chicken
